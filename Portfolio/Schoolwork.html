Fear Destroyer

A group project assigned as our final. (Please click on the image to be sent to the site.) The concept was designing a website game that connected to a google forms page. where players would add to an ever growing list of peoples fears. each fear was rated 1-5 and using this knowledge we changed the sprites of the clouds to be darker the worse the fear.

The controls are simple, please use the arrow keys to control the ball of light and fly into the clouds flying around to disperse them.
